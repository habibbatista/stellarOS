What is stellarOS?

stellarOS is a custom-monolithic kernel-based Operating System designed from the ground up for lightning-fast performance, daily dependability, and high-performance gaming.

Performance Fast performance on low-end devices. Optimized to preserve hardware resources.
Creativity Creativity-focused first-party programs developed native to the OS.
Design Beautiful interface layouts crafted with precision, lightweight on processing threads.
Control Wide variety of deep system customization options to construct your space.
Intelligence Seamless cloud-based Artificial Intelligence utilities using Google Gemini 3.5 Flash.
Simplicity User-friendly operating flow built alongside a fast, painless installer.
And much more!
